The Best K-Dramas According to Ana

My top 3 dramas of all-time.

Signal

This is one of the best dramas I've ever watched. Even thought it's quite fictional it really portrayed the life of a detective and his love adventures so well.

Partners for justice

This one has 2 seasons and it shows the teamwork between a prosecutor and a NFS doctor who try to make the world a better place and solve all of the crimes.

Voice

THis drama has 4 seasons and it is about a policewoman who has a hearing condition that helps her hear every little sound around her. With her ability and the help of other detectives they form a Golden Time Team that save the 112 callers in crucial situations.
